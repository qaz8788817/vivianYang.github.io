2023/03 - 2023/08 與長庚大學職能治療學系的同學共同發想與實作RehaBreathe系統！

### 動機
Fatigue is a major problematic symptom that significantly affects COVID long-haulers’ ability to work and perform daily tasks. 

To fill this gap, our team developed RehaBreathe, an intelligent system for managing users’ energy efficiently, while simultaneously offering useful strategies tailored to users’ daily tasks and physiological conditions.

### 方法
Each user of the LineBot Official Account is assigned a unique User ID (UID), which we utilize to bind smartwatch users with their designated emergency contacts. Real-time physiological data monitored by the smartwatch is synchronized to a cloud platform. The system performs data analytics at 5-second intervals to detect potential anomalies. Upon identifying any irregularities, LineBot automatically triggers alerts to both the user and their family members, advising immediate rest or medical attention. Furthermore, the system generates comprehensive long-term health reports, including longitudinal trends in heart rate and blood oxygen levels (SpO2).
